UX CASE STUDY

Snacks Ordering App at Movie Theatre

Creating Snacks Ordering App at Movie theatre to satisfy customer requirement and also to reduce the customer inconvenience of waiting in a queue. This online system makes a greaer change in their business and it's improve their sales and new customer.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

What is Snacks Ordering App at Movie Theatre?

A Mobile App which is used to buy snacks in movie theatres. Through this you can view the list of snacks,Choose,Customize and Order as per your need from your seat itself.

Problem Statement

When you are watching a movie in a theatre, if you feel like have some snacks you need to go to the snacks counter and may wait for a longtime for your snacks. It makes the customer to get frustrated that they miss their movie scenes.

Solution

Design an Snacks ordering app for Movie Theatre that allows users to satisfy their requirement and also to reduce the user inconvenience of waiting in a queue.

Discover Phase

Discover Phase is a journey of discovery with the aim of getting to know user pain points, frustration and to gain an understanding of the user.

User Research Summary

I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to understand the users I’m designing for and their needs. A primary user group identified through research was people who don’t buy snacks at movie theatre because of waiting in a queue . Research also revealed that waiting in a queue was not the only factor limiting users to buy snacks at movie theatre. Other user problems included missed their favourite scenes while waiting in a queue long time, sometimes their favourite snacks get out of stock .
